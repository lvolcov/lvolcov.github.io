How to play:

  • You will receive four random items and have to answer how many of each one you want to buy, you have to spend at least 90% of his money.
  • You have 5 chances to submit your answer and the game will tell you if you should spend more or less.
  • If you submit and your answer spends between 90% to 100% of his fortune, you win!
  • If at the end of the fifth submit you don't reach it, you lose.
